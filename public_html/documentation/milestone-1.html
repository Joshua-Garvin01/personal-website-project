PWP Milestone-1

Purpose

The purpose of this website will be to showcase my skills in front end web developments.

Audience

This website is designed to attract attention from small business owners who don't want to deal with large overcharging companies.

Goal

To provide a professional showcased platform with my skills & information on display so I can be contacted and connected with small businesses or partners.

Persona

Jim

Role: Small business owner. Landscaper with a crew of 12.

Devices

  • Smart phone (iPhone)
  • Table (iPad)
  • PC (Windows)
  • Browser (Chrome & Safari)

Frustrations

I'm so tired of dealing with large web development companies that are expensive to begin with, then they're constantly over charging for maintenance services. When I tried to go cheaper, there was no professionalism.

Goals

I really want someone who is reasonably priced, professional, and local. As well as someone who can convey information about my company in a clean and concise way.

User Story

As the user, I need to quickly determine weather the provider can offer professional and concise information for my small business.
